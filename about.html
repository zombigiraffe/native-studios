More about myself and my studio

I've always had a strong passion for music since I was very young. I've been playing guitar for 15 years, bass for 10 years, drums (off and on) for 10 years, screaming vocals for 8 years, and got into audio production 10 years ago. I've produced many songs for many bands/artists, including my own, spanning across a wide array of genres. I am a strong advocate for the ideology of "it's all about your ear, not your gear". My studio setup may not have all the biggest or fanciest analog gear, but that has never stopped me from making killer songs with my "in the box" approach. I always make sure to provide a comfortable, easy-going studio experience in order to ensure the recording process is seamless and fun, while still providing a product with great quality and a very affordable price. My clients have often told me I'm a fast worker, hard worker, talented, and easy to work with. I always make sure that the band or artist is nothing but happy and ecstatic by the time we're done working together. Also, my 11+ years of song-writing experience for all of my past and current bands definitely comes in handy when working on a project. So whether it be producing, song-writing, recording, editing, mixing, or mastering, I'd love to work with you! Make sure to reach out for any of your music/audio needs!
